With the manga closing in on 200 volumes, the anime with 12 episodes in it's 1stseason and an incoming 2nd season this anime has won over the hearts of it's fans. Tsuredure/Tsurezure Children is a romantic comedy about multiple highschool students struggling to confess to their crushes, which I'm sure we can all relate to at one point. The anime does a great job of connecting with it's audience by relating with us. We can laugh at what they are doing but have we really done any better?
